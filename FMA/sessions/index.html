Surrounded by Idiots by Thomas Erikson
introduces the concept of four personality
types, each represented by a color: Red
(dominant and assertive), Yellow (social and creative),
Green (calm and empathetic), and Blue
(analytical and precise). The author explains
how understanding these types can improve
communication and reduce conflict, as people tend
to perceive and respond to situations in ways
that align with their dominant personality traits.
The book offers practical advice on how to interact
effectively with each type in both personal and professional settings.
