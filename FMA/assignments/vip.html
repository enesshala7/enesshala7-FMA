Eminem - The Rap God

Marshall Bruce Mathers III, known professionally as Eminem, is one of the greatest rappers of all time. He revolutionized the hip-hop industry with his lyrical genius and storytelling ability.

Achievements

  • 15 Grammy Awards
  • Over 220 million records sold worldwide
  • First rapper to win an Academy Award (for "Lose Yourself")
  • Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2022
